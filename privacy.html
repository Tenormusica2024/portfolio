This site uses Google AdSense for advertising. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits. Users can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

We will not disclose personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect collected information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as necessary. Any changes will be posted on this page.
